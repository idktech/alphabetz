Our Services

Hello from Alphabetz Daycare

Alphabetz Daycare offers intuitive learning through activities and fun! Your child will never be bored because we are always having fun! We have lots of fun games, activities, puzzles, toys, disco balls with music for dancing and so much more! We work with all families and accept DSHS!

Certified In:

  • CPR, First Aid
  • Food handlers permit
  • HIV Aids, 30 hours STARS Training
  • Blood Borne Pathogens, Early achievers program
  • Background Check by DEL (Department of Early Learning)

Infants

Our classroom offers a warm secure location where infants receive personal, individualized attention and care. The stimulating classroom environment helps form a foundation for later learning.

Toddlers

Energy and excitement are a natural part of growth and learning. We channel that energy into positive learning experiences, giving toddlers room to move and explore. We go beyond daycare to provide nurturing guidance as they develop physical skills and master emerging milestones.

Pre-School

To engage and nurture your child, we provide a balance of freedom and instruction. Our early childhood education classroom is designed as a learning community, where your child not only builds skills vital to success in kindergarten, but also gains respect for others, and a strong sense of confidence.

About our Daycare

Alphabetz DayCare is an at-home day care facility providing a fun learning environment for children to bloom.

What matters to us at Alphabetz Daycare is simple: Your child! We provide strong social and educational foundations that prepare your child for success in elementary school and beyond.

Our schools and classrooms are designed to give children room to grow, room to share, and room to be themselves. Here, children work together, have constructive fun together, and discover how enjoyable learning can be.

infrastructure

The daycare and learning infrastructure is designed in a way to be both fun, interactive and malleable in way to accommodate all children.

Safety First

Everything we do at the daycare is centered around having fun and being safe!

Activities

My focus is geared towards developing the cognitive, emotional, fine motor, social and gross skills of the children in my care.
